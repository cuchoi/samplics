Population parameters estimation¶

samplics.estimation.expansion¶

Expansion module

The module implements the taylor-based variance estimation methods. There are several good books that provide the the taylor-based approximations of the sample variance. Some of these reference books are Cochran, W.G. (1977) 1, Kish, L. (1965) 2, Lohr, S.L. (2010) 3, and Wolter, K.M. (2007) 4.

1

Cochran, W.G. (1977), Sampling Techniques, 3rd edn., Jonh Wiley & Sons, Inc.

2

Kish, L. (1965), Survey Sampling, Jonh Wiley & Sons, Inc.

3

Lohr, S.L. (2010), Sampling: Design and Analysis, 2nd edn., Cengage Learning, Inc.

4

Wolter, K.M. (2007), Introduction to Variance Estimation, 2nd edn., Springer-Verlag New York, Inc
